Research Interests

1.Securing the Internet of Things (IoT) Landscape

I'm fascinated by the potential of IoT devices, but I'm equally concerned about their vulnerabilities. I want to explore how to ensure the security and privacy of interconnected devices in our homes, workplaces, and cities. This involves studying ways to encrypt communications, authenticate devices, and prevent unauthorized access to our personal data.

2.Harnessing Machine Learning to Detect Cyber Threats

I'm drawn to the idea of using advanced technologies like machine learning to stay ahead of cyber threats. My goal is to dive into the world of algorithms and data analysis, developing smart systems that can identify unusual patterns in network traffic or user behavior. This way, we can predict and prevent attacks before they even happen.

3. Exploring Blockchain's Impact on Cybersecurity

Blockchain seems like a game-changer, and I'm curious about how it can revolutionize cybersecurity. I aim to unravel the potential benefits and pitfalls of integrating blockchain technology into existing security practices. This might involve studying how decentralized ledgers can thwart data breaches, ensure secure digital identities, and transform the way we authenticate transactions.
